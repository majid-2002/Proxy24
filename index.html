PROXY LEGACY

PROXY 2022 was a defining moment for Government Engineering College Thrissur's Computer Science Department, launching us into the national tech spotlight. With 500+ registrations from all over India and powered by the incredible support of Qburst and KeyValue, this flagship event wasn't just another tech fest, it was the start of something bigger.

Spanning 36 hours of non-stop innovation and adventure, PROXY 2022 was more than just a hackathon; it was a Halloween-themed, fun-filled event that fused creativity with real-world problem-solving. Beyond the excitement, it became a career launchpad, with top talent catching the eye of leading companies. And the momentum is only building from here!
